\section{Question}

Let $S$ be quantity of soda, $M$ be quantity of movies.

Utility defined as

\[ U = 1S + 2M\]

Budget of \$25, with $P_S = 5$ and $P_M = 10$

\[25 = S * 5 + M * 10\]

Performing some algebra on the budget function,

\begin{align*}
25 &= 5S + 10M \\
S &= 5 - 2M
\end{align*}

Substituting this into the utility function,

\begin{align*}
U &= 1S + 2M \\
U &= 5 -2M + 2M \\
U &= 5
\end{align*}

When budget changes to \$50

\[50 = 5S + 10M \]
